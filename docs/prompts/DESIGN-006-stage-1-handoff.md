`MainScreen` owns the visible `PageWidget` list and builds AppBar/FAB chrome from each page controller. Its field-held `StationListController` and `RolePlaysController` must be reused because their views share notifier state and duplicate instances would create duplicate subscriptions.
`ProgramView`, `StationListView`, `RolePlaysView`, and `TeamsView` are list bodies that keep local expansion state and read `MasterDetailScope`; their contextual chrome lives on controllers. Stage 1 can keep the Program segment selection on `ProgramPageControllerBase`, which outlives `ProgramView` rebuilds and is reachable from `MainScreen`.

Stage 1 landed with controller-owned `ProgramSegment` state, a width-aware segmented switcher, reused structural list bodies, contextual FAB/AppBar delegation, and segment-aware wide empty panes. Stage 2 can remove the redundant root tabs while retaining the field-held station, roleplay, and team controllers passed into the Program controller and view.
