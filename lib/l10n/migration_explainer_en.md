# About the migration

## Why are we moving?

The web app is moving to a new domain – **web.ringdrill.app** – for better performance, stability, and easier updates. The new domain will have its own dedicated app hosting.

## What changes for you?

The existing app at `ringdrill.app` will stop receiving updates. The new app is installed from **web.ringdrill.app** as a fresh PWA — just like you did when you installed this one.

## How to migrate your plans

1. Tap **"Export all my plans"** here in the app (or in the banner at the top).
2. Open **web.ringdrill.app** and install the new app.
3. Select **Import** and choose the ZIP file you just downloaded.
4. All your plans are now available in the new app.

## What happens to my data here?

Your plans are stored in the browser on `ringdrill.app` and will not disappear automatically. You can export them again here until you clear browser data for this domain. After the next update, a migration page on the new domain will help you transfer directly.
